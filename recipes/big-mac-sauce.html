Big Mac-Sauce

Description

This is the industry secret recipe for Mcdonald's Big Mac. Now you can make one at home with indgredients below.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup of sweet picked relish
  • 2 tablespoons of DUsseldorf mustard (but regular mustard will work)
  • 1 teaspoon of white wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon of onion powders
  • 1 teaspoon of granulated garlic
  • 1 1/4 teaspoons of paprika
  • 1/8 teaspoon of white pepper

Steps

  1. Place all ingredients together in a small bowl and whisk to combine.
  2. Can be enjoyed immediately, but the flavor is best when allowed to sit in the refrigerator overnight. Store in an air-tight container.
